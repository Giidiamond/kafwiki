Earth

Earth is the main planet of universe-21122 and is the planet where Kasen, Pacman, Sabrina, Brian, and the other residents of the Papergutory came from.
The planet (and to some extent universe-21122) is the only place not affected by the Almighty. It is unknown
why the Almighty can't influence Earth, despite the fact the Almighty was able to impact
universe-21122's dimensions, i.e., Heaven, Hell, Papergutory and some other unnamed dimensions. Due to this unknown fact, the Almighty
mostly can't access Earth. Keyword, mostly.

Current state

Earth is currently in a fine state. The people of the planet are in an alright position in terms of money,
food, technology, and environments. But even with that, the people go into infighting due to different perspectives, opinions, and beliefs.
